The Aviation detachment of the 212th Attack Battalion is known by the callsign Wampa. Wampa’s main priority in operations is to assist infantry in completing their objectives. Wampa’s tasks consist of reconnaissance, transportation and close air support. To manage these tasks effectively, Wampa’s pilots are highly trained in their opponent's capabilities, accurate map reading, controlling their aircraft and Utilizing their ordinance.



To join Wampa, you're required to have the Rotary Wing Qual, but can obtain JTAC/TECH/FXW to run additional roles/vehicles, click the link for more information on Wampa.

The Mechanized detachment of the 212th Attack Battalion is known by the callsign Phantom. Phantom is an Armored Transport and Fire Support detachment comprised of two separate branches, Mechanized and Armored. Phantom supports the infantry in a number of various ways, including troop transport, enemy asset denial, and overwhelming fire support.



To join Phantom, members require the Gunnery Qual, but can also earn the Mechanized & Armor qual to get access to more assets, click the link for more information on Phantom.

The Reconnaissance detachment of the 212th Attack Battalion is known by the callsign Poltergeist. Poltergeist specializes in providing recon of enemy locations, fortifications and movements. Poltergeist is able to engage targets from extreme distances when called upon to support the main elements of the Platoon, and has access to the BARC speeders that allow troopers to move around much more effectively. The ARF team can also act in multiple instances as an additional force multiplier to the Platoon by acting as light skirmishers for flanking, or preventing flanks of the Platoon.



To join Poltergeist you must be SCT+ and have the quals Marksman and CLS (Combat Lifesaver), then earn the ARF Qual. The Airborne qual is also recommended later as certain insertions require the use of the Airborne qual's jump pack, click the link for more information on Poltergeist.

The Artillery and Modular Weapons Team of the 212th Attack Battalion is known by the callsign Revenant. Revenant is a support detachment that focuses on indirect fire support and heavy modular weapons teams to provide fire support to general infantry. Fire Support Group currently utilizes the Grenade Machine Gun, the Heavy Blaster, and the Anti-Air PLX. Artillerymen operate both Mortars and the AV-7.



Recruits are required to obtain the MWT qual at minimum, but are recommended to obtain ARTY to utilize Revenant's Mortars, click the link for more information on Revenant.

The Airborne detachment of the 212th Attack Battalion is known by the callsign Spectre. Each platoon has a Spectre squad as part of it's composition. Spectre specializes in fast attack, highly mobile infantry gameplay. With their jump packs, Spectre is able to drop behind enemy lines and engage enemies either to the rear or use them to flank positions with their increased speed. Spectre, using their jump packs, is able to close the gap between themselves and the enemy, allowing them to excel in CQC combat environments. They are also able to quickly redeploy to vulnerable flanks.



To join Spectre, members must first have 1 standard qual to take the Airborne qual, after completing the Airborne qual, members may contact a Team lead or Squad lead in any Platoon as long as a slot is open, click the link for more information on Spectre.

The Republic Commandos of the 212th Attack Battalion are known by the callsign Foxtrot Group. Foxtrot Group members serve as a multirole asset used to provide an additional layer of versatility and combat power alongside ground forces. The Commandos in-game role are to act as a versatile, adaptable and rapidly deployable shock trooper able to bolster the infantry forces during engagements or as a jack of all trades when dealing with a variety of threats faced by ground forces in operations.



To join Foxtrot, members must be SCT+ with 16+ attendances, as well as all standard quals and Demo (Medic recommended for members wishing to get the medic slot) Once the recruit has the quals, they must obtain the Republic Commando qual and possess no Disciplinary actions or Warnings within the past 6 months, click the link for more information on Foxtrot.

The Advanced Recon Commandos of the 212th Attack Battalion are known by the callsign Banshee. Banshee members are primarily attached to the regular infantry element, acting as a force multiplier, however can be tasked with eliminating enemy installations out of the way of the main fighting force, or HVT capture and extraction.



To join Banshee, members must have all Standard Quals, and are recommended having Medic/ARF/Demo, after that they must be billited in an infantry fireteam without an ARC trooper, and a squad with less than 2 ARCs in it (Talk with Banshee Leadership or a Newcomer Liason to help find a suitable slot) Following that, recruits must earn a reommendation from their squad leader and another NCO (SNCO if your squad leader is not SNCO) And if approved, members may attend ARC Training, upon completion members will be welcomed to the Banshee detachment, click the link for more information on Banshee.
